HTML Tags

These are some of the tags I have learnt

Headings 'h'

Creates headings for the most important information and contains levels from level 1 to 6.

Footer 'footer'

Contains all the information for a well ending on the page.

Quote 'q'

Creates an embeded quote.

Paragraph 'p'

Gives meaning to the information of a text in form of a paragraph and creates space at the start as well as at the end, to separate the information.

Anchor 'a'

Creates a hyperlink that links the current page with other URL content.
